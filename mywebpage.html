About

Buren is my family name and I hailed from the islands of Kiribati. Where is that? Is the response I usually get. We are scattered in the middle of the Pacific Ocean. Fun Fact: We may be one of the tiniest nations but we the only country to be situated in the four hemispheres!
New Zealand has been my home for 10 years now. I have a mixed experience in terms of work, ranging from health, horticulture, construction and the usual fun labour here and there to make meets end.
I've been fascinated by technology but never really got a chance to dive in and completely take advantage of studying it while available in NZ.
Mission Ready has reignited that interest in me and it gives me hope and inspiration to stay curious and be involved in the ever changing technology and its effects on our everyday life
